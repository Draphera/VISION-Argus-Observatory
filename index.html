Safety boundary.

The public layer is designed to be useful without becoming dangerous.

This public observatory does not contain raw CAD files, HPGL/HPGL2 source files, VNF files, coordinates, local paths, customer filenames, production machine output, or customer geometry.
Intake requests start a human quarantine workflow. They do not upload files, execute TITANO, certify files, update LEXICON, or expose private ARGUS analysis.
