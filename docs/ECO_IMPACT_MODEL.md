# Eco-Impact Model

ARGUS eco-impact metrics are parametric estimates.

They are not certification claims.

## Inputs

- idle distance saved
- unit-to-millimeter factor
- rapid travel speed
- idle power
- electricity carbon intensity

## Outputs

- estimated production time recovered
- estimated mechanical energy saved
- estimated industrial CO2 avoided

Fabric savings are not computed unless a reliable area delta model is available.
